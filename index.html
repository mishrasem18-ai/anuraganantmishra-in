Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Analytics?

Digital Analytics is the practice of collecting, measuring and interpreting digital interactions to improve user experiences, marketing effectiveness and business outcomes.

What is MarTech Architecture?

MarTech Architecture connects analytics, customer data, personalization and activation platforms into a scalable technology ecosystem.

What is Customer Journey Analytics?

Customer Journey Analytics helps organizations understand customer interactions across channels to improve experiences and decision making.

Why is Privacy-first Measurement important?

Privacy-first measurement supports responsible data collection while maintaining useful analytics and compliance with modern privacy expectations.

How does AI improve Digital Analytics?
